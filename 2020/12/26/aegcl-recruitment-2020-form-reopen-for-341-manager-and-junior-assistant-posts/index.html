AEGCL Recruitment 2020: Form Reopen For 341 Manager and Junior Assistant Posts

  • Post author:Sohel
  • Post published:December 26, 2020
  • Post category:Govt Jobs
  • Post comments:0 Comments

AEGCL Recruitment 2020: Assam Power Distribution Company (APDCL), Assam Electricity Grid Corporation Limited (AEGCL)  has released a notification for the recruitment of the Assistant & Junior Manager @aegcl.co.in. The online application form is available for submission from 09th to 18th December 2020. Eligible and interested candidates can apply for the recruitment in the prescribed format as given below. Check the details, Application Form, and other important details.

Update: The online application is reopened from 24th to 31st December 2020. This is to address the issue of problem faced by candidates in filling the application.

AEGCL Recruitment Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

  • Candidates should possess Full-time B.E/B.Tech (Relevant Disciplines) or Diploma course in Engineering from a recognized University/ Institution.

Age Limit

  • Assistant Manager: 21 to 44 Yrs.
