Indian Railway Rail Wheel Plant Recruitment 2020: Apply Online For 70 Apprentice

Posted byManish26th December 202026th December 2020Leave a comment on Indian Railway Rail Wheel Plant Recruitment 2020: Apply Online For 70 Apprentice

Indian Railway Rail Wheel Plant Recruitment 2020: Indian Railway Rail Wheel Plant has invited online applications for Trainee Apprentice posts @portal.mhrdnats.gov.in. Check notice and last date to apply. Candidates who fulfill the eligibility criteria shall apply online from the link provided below. Read the given article for more details.

Indian Railway Rail Wheel Plant Recruitment

Indian Railway Rail Wheel Plant Recruitment has released the online application of Trainee Apprentice posts. Given below is an overview of Indian Railway Rail Wheel Plant Recruitment 2020.

Download Indian Railway Rail Wheel Plant Recruitment Official Notification 2020

Indian Railway Rail Wheel Plant Apprentice Vacancy Details

Check the details of vacancies in the table:

Indian Railway Rail Wheel Plant Apprentice Eligibility Criteria

Education Qualification

  • Tech/B.Sc. in Mechanical Engg.: Four years Bachelor’s Degree in Mechanical/Industrial/Automobile/Production Engineering from a Recognized University/Institute approved by AICTE.
  • Tech/B.Sc. in Electrical Engg.: Four years Bachelor degree in (i) Electrical/Electronics & Power Engineering or (ii) Combination of any sub stream of Basics streams of Electrical from recognized University/Institute approved by AICTE.
  • Tech/B.Sc. in Electronics Instrumentation/Computer/IT Engg.: Four years Bachelor’s Degree in Instrumentation/Electronics/Electrical & Electronics/ Electronics & Communication/Telecommunication/Computer Science/1.T. Engg. from a Recognized University/Institute approved by AICTE.
  • Diploma in Mechanical Engg.: Full-time regular Diploma in Mechanical/ Industrial/Automobile/Production Engineering from University/Institute recognized by AICTE.
  • Diploma in Electrical Engg.: Full-time regular Diploma in Electrical/Electronics & Power Engineering or a combination of any sub stream of basic streams of Electrical/Electronics from recognized University/Institute.
  • Diploma in Electronics Instrumentation/Computer/IT Engg.: Full time regular Diploma in Instrumentation/Electronics/Electrical & Electronics/Electronics & Communication/Telecommunication/Computer Science/1.T. Engg. from recognized University/Institute.

Age (As of 01st October 2020)

  • Minimum: 15 Years
  • Maximum: 24 Years

Indian Railway Rail Wheel Plant Apprentice Steps to Apply Online

  1. Click on the link that is given below.
  2. The candidates will have to first themselves.
  3. Then a registration no. and a password will be provided.
  4. Log in and complete the application form.
  5. Fill in all the details.
  6. Upload the documents in the required format size and format.
  7. Finally submit the application form online.
  8. Take a print out of the application form for future reference.

Apprentice Online Application

The online application is available till 14th January 2021.

Indian Railway Rail Wheel Plant Apprentice Online Application Form

Indian Railway Rail Wheel Plant Apprentice Online Application Form stands invalid if:

  • Providing wrong information in the application form
  • Providing misleading information
  • More than one application form is submitted by one candidate. Only one of the applications will be considered for the final submission.
  • Non-submission of application fee
  • The submission of the Indian Railway Rail Wheel Plant Apprentice Recruitment by post is not acceptable.
